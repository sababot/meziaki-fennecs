THE DESERT

An Exceptional project started by 4 NFT enthusiasts, launching on the Solana Blockchain, revolving around the “Fennec fox”-Algeria’s National animal.

Around the clock, the team prepares a launch of 1809 fennec NFTs. With a plethora of distinct traits and a multitude of backgrounds- we voluntarily undertake this challenge to depict the abundance of originality in the life of a fennec.

A composed selected team with copious skills to grant access to opportunities and new gaming experiences.

With the upcoming release of our IOS game, we undertake this enjoyable-yet challenging task to represent the might of this great animal and his expertise when it comes to life adaptability in the most vast and arid desert in the world.

We've come to stay.

FAQ

What is the mint price?

It is yet to be determined. However, what is certain is that whitelist will get a lower mint price than public.

How many fennecs are there in the collection?

There is a total of 1809 fennecs, the year of the resurgence of the fennecs. An additional 1809 baby fennecs will be created in a future collection. Breeding two fennecs will create one legendary baby fennec.

What charity are we donating money to?

Two members will be donating 10% of the profit they earn to the "Sahara Conservation Charity" which will use the money to conserve endangered species in the Saharan desert such as the fennec.

Where can I buy Meziaki Fennecs?

Meziaki Fennecs will be listed in Opensea as well as Magic Eden. Be wary of unofficial marketplaces and imitations.

Will we sell out?

Most certainly, don't miss it.
