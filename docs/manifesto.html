MANIFESTO

Is that a Fennec? I think i know,
Its owner, the desert, quite happy though
Full of life and lack of mercy
Distant mirage, water murky.

Barren and deadly, I observe the dunes
Whose life is in their hands anew
With heat and rest, a guide to stay
To prevent you from ever turning away.

Ears like an elephant, promises he keeps
Strong he is, despite his appearance meek
Love he has, Loyalty he demands
Without him even the best are damned.
